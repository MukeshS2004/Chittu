Friends Of Farmers

  • Friends Of Farmers refer to an individuals or organizations that provide support and assistance to farmers
  • We can offer finanical assistance in the form of grants,loans or subsidies that helps farmers to cover expenses for seeds ,equipment
  • We can assist farmers in acessing markets and negotiating for fair prices for their products

Investments

Analysis of Investment

  • Government invests in FPOs(Farmer Producer organizations),And got several positive outcomes and benefits for both agricultural sector and overall economy
  • Funds allocated to FPOs used to invest in Modern farming techniques,technology that leads to higher yields
  • Theses investments help FPOs to build stronger and organized farmers collectives, enabling them to negotiate for better prices.
